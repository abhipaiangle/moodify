Hacktoberfest is great. This little change is a change in frontend of a application xD and I get a T-shirt! Yayy!

The target of the project was to build an app which is a music recommender that takes in your picture and then predicts your mood and accordingly suggests a music playlist for you on spotify.

Flow:

  • The user must generate their authorisation tokens by selecting the right checkboxes as mentioned in the video tutorial.
  • The user must copy the personalized token and paste it in the Moodify window.
  • The web application Moodify allows the user to click a picture of himself/ herself or if he /she is confident about their current mood, can directly select a mood out of our 4 default moods
  • If the user wants his/her suggested playlist to be added to their Spotify account, then they must click on the corresponding check box.
  • Now, the user can visit their Spotify account and listen to their personalised playlist!.
